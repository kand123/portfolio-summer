About My Education and Career

My name is Kristin and I'm an aspiring web developer. I've always been detail-oriented in my work, which previously consisted of research, editing, and technical writing. One day I started doing research on different career paths and started falling in love with web development. I started teaching myself to code with online resources in 2016, but wasn't sure whether I preferred web design or development. While I enjoy designing, I've discovered that my heart belongs to development and the magic of making those designs come alive.
