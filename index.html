About My Education and Career

My name is Kristin and I’m an aspiring web developer. I’ve always been detail-oriented in my work, which consisted of research, editing, and technical writing until I fell in love with development. While I do enjoy designing, my heart belongs to development and the magic of making those designs come alive.

My present educational goals include learning more outside the classroom and pushing myself beyond the boundaries of my assignments. I am attending Utah Valley University because it gives me a broad-based knowledge of web practices, but I know I need to dive deep if I am going to be successful. This field moves too fast and there are far too many technologies to learn them all in the classroom.
